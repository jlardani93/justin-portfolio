About Me

I am currently attending the Epicodus programming school in Seattle, Washington. Prior to becoming a student here, I spent two years teaching Math and Science in Philadelphia while pursuing my Masters at the University of Pennsylvania. And before that, I studied Education, Mandarin, and Public Policy as an undergraduate at Brown University. The field of Education is, without a doubt, my passion. I hope to be able to use the skills I am currently learning at Epicodus to effect positive change within the American educational system.

When I'm not programming, I'm generally either outdoors running, hiking or camping; reading; cooking; or playing video games. Though I will likely not have any time for doing so in the foreseeable future, what I really love to invest all of my extra time and money in is travelling. I have been to China, many countries throughout Europe, several countries in South America, Canada, and Mexico. I also just recently completed a cross-country road trip from Philadelphia to Seattle with some friends. I'm hoping to find an opportunity soon to fly up to Alaska to see the northern lights.
